🦉 Professor Owl’s 40-Step 1040 Beginner Guide

🦉 Welcome! I will guide you step-by-step through your entire 1040 tax return.

🧍 Section 1: Personal Info

1. Filing status (single / married / head of household)
2. Your full name
3. Spouse name (if applicable)
4. Social Security Number
5. Dependents
🦉 Always double-check your SSN — mistakes cause delays.

💼 Section 2: Income

6. W-2 wages (job income)
7. Interest income
8. Dividend income
9. IRA distributions
10. Retirement income
11. Social Security income
12. Capital gains
13. Other income (freelance / side jobs)
14. Total income
🦉 Add ALL income sources — even small ones matter.

📉 Section 3: Adjustments

15. Student loan interest
16. Educator expenses
17. HSA contributions
18. IRA deductions
19. Self-employment deductions
20. Total adjustments

📊 Section 4: Taxable Income

21. Adjusted Gross Income (AGI)
22. Standard deduction
23. Itemized deduction option
24. Final taxable income

💰 Section 5: Tax Calculation

25. Federal tax (IRS tables)
26. Additional taxes
27. Special tax rules (if applicable)
28. Total tax owed

🎯 Section 6: Credits

29. Child tax credit
30. Education credits
31. Earned income credit
32. Other credits
33. Total credits

💳 Section 7: Payments

34. Federal tax withheld
35. Estimated payments
36. Refundable credits
37. Total payments

📦 Section 8: Final Result

38. Refund amount
39. Amount owed
40. Final balance

🖊️ Section 9: Signature & Filing

41. Taxpayer signature
42. Spouse signature (if joint)
43. Filing date
44. Identity verification PIN
45. Submit return (efile or mail)
