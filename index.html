Form 1040 — Add Up Your Income (Lines 1–8)

💬 Professor Hootington: “This is like counting all your money in one place.”

  • Line 1a – Wages, Salaries, Tips: From your W-2
  • Line 2a – Tax-Exempt Interest
  • Line 2b – Taxable Interest
  • Line 3a – Qualified Dividends
  • Line 3b – Ordinary Dividends
  • Line 4a – IRA Distributions
  • Line 4b – Taxable IRA
  • Line 5a – Pensions & Annuities
  • Line 5b – Taxable Pensions
  • Line 6a – Social Security
  • Line 6b – Taxable Social Security
  • Line 7 – Other Income
  • Line 8 – Total Income

Step 1 — Gather Your Papers

  • W-2 from your job
  • 1099 forms
  • Receipts
  • Last year’s return

Step 2 — Fill in Your Personal Info

  • Name, address, SSN
  • Spouse info
  • Dependents

Step 3 — Adjustments

  • Student loan interest
  • IRA contributions

Step 4 — Deductions

  • Standard or itemized deduction

Step 5 — Calculate Tax

  • Total tax owed

Step 6 — Credits & Payments

  • Withholding
  • Credits

Step 7 — Refund or Amount Owed

  • If you paid too much → refund
  • If not enough → pay balance
