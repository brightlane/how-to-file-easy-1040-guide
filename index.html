🦉 Professor Owl’s Easy Tax Guide

✅ PART 1 — Get Your Papers Ready

Step 1: Income Papers

  • W-2 = money from your job
  • 1099 = extra money (side jobs, bank interest)
  • Other money (tips, small jobs)
Tip: Put everything in one folder.

Step 2: Deductions (Things that save money)

  • Charity donations
  • Medical bills
  • School costs
Tip: Keep receipts!

Step 3: Personal Info

  • Social Security numbers
  • Bank account
Tip: Check numbers carefully.

✅ PART 2 — Filling Out Your Taxes

Step 4: Add Your Income

Combine all your income sources.

Step 5: Adjust Your Income

Subtract eligible adjustments.

Step 6: Find Your AGI

AGI = Total income minus adjustments

Step 7: Deductions

Choose standard or itemized deductions.

Step 8: Calculate Tax

Compute total tax owed.

Step 9: Credits & Payments

Apply tax credits to reduce tax.

Step 10: Refund or Owe

Determine refund or balance due.
