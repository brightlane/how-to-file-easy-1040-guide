📝 Quick Tax Cheat Sheet

  • Step 1: Gather W-2, 1099, and receipts.
  • Step 2: Box 1 on W-2 is your total money earned.
  • Step 3: Deductions lower your AGI (Adjusted Gross Income).
  • Step 4: File online for a refund in ~21 days.

🦉 How to Read Your W-2

  • Box 1: Wages, tips, other compensation.
  • Box 2: Federal income tax withheld (Money already paid).
  • Box 3: Social Security wages.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a tax refund take?

If you file online and choose direct deposit, most refunds arrive within 21 days.

What if I missed the April deadline?

You can file an extension to move your deadline to October 15th, but taxes owed are still due in April.
