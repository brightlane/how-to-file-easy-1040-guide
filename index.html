🦉 Professor Owl's Easy Tax Guide (2026 Edition)

✅ PART 1 — Get Your Papers Ready (15 Minutes)

Step 1: Income Papers

  • W-2 = Money from your job (employer sends by Jan 31)
  • 1099-NEC/MISC = Side jobs, freelance, bank interest
  • Other: Tips, small jobs, crypto transactions
🦉 Owl Tip: Create one folder called "2025 Taxes" and dump everything there.

Step 2: Deductions (Money Savers)

  • Charity donations (receipts needed)
  • Medical bills over 7.5% of income
  • Student loan interest ($2,500 max)
  • Home office (if self-employed)
🦉 Owl Tip: Take photos of receipts with your phone!

Step 3: Personal Info

  • Social Security numbers (you + dependents)
  • Bank routing/account for direct deposit
  • 2024 AGI (from last year's return)
🦉 Owl Tip: Double-check SSNs - one digit wrong = rejected return!

✅ PART 2 — Filling Out Your 1040 (30 Minutes)

Step 4: Total Income (Lines 1-11)

  • Line 1: W-2 wages (Box 1)
  • Line 2b: Taxable interest
  • Line 3b: Dividends
  • Line 7: Capital gains
  • Line 8: Other income (side hustles)
🦉 Owl Math: Add all income boxes together = Line 11 total.

Step 5: Adjustments (Line 12-15)

  • Student loan interest
  • IRA contributions
  • Health Savings Account
  • Self-employment tax (½)
🦉 Owl Tip: These lower your income before tax rates apply!

Step 6: AGI (Line 15)

AGI = Total Income − Adjustments
This is your "taxable income number" that determines your tax bracket.

Step 7: Deductions (Line 16-19)

  • Standard Deduction (2026):
    Single: $15,000 | Married: $30,000 | Head of Household: $22,500
  • Itemized: State tax + mortgage + charity + medical
🦉 Owl Rule: Pick whichever is BIGGER to save more money!

Step 8: Taxable Income → Tax Owed

Taxable Income = AGI − Deductions
Use the tax table or software calculates automatically.

Step 9: Credits = FREE MONEY (Lines 20+)

  • Child Tax Credit: $2,000 per child under 17
  • EITC: Earned Income Credit (low/moderate income)
  • Education credits for college tuition
🦉 Owl Secret: Credits reduce tax dollar-for-dollar (better than deductions)!

Step 10: Payments Already Made

  • W-2 Box 2 (federal withholding)
  • Estimated payments
  • Refundable credits

Step 11: Refund or Owe

  • Line 35 > Line 24: You get refund!
  • Line 24 > Line 35: You owe money
