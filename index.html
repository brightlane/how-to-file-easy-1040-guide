Class is in session! Let's examine your return line-by-line.

Start Filing Now

Section 1: Who are you?

Lines 1-7: Personal Details

▼

This is basic data entry. Name, address, and your Social Security Number.

"Pay attention! Your name must match your Social Security Card EXACTLY or the IRS will reject your return."

Section 2: The Money

Line 1a: Wages & Salaries (W-2)

▼

This is the total from Box 1 of all your W-2 forms.

"If you had three jobs, you add all three Box 1 totals together. Simple math, students!"

Section 3: The Cuts

Line 12: Standard Deduction

▼

The "Free Pass" from the IRS. It lowers your taxable income automatically.

"Most of you will take the Standard Deduction. It's the IRS saying, 'The first few thousand you made is on the house!'"

Section 4: The Result

Line 34: Your Refund

▼

If you paid more than you owed, this is the amount coming back to you.

"The best part of class! Make sure your bank routing number is correct for Direct Deposit."
