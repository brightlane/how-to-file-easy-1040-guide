Step 1 — Gather Your Papers

  • W‑2 from your job
  • 1099 forms for freelance or interest
  • Receipts for deductions (education, charity)
  • Last year’s tax return (if you have it)

Step 2 — Fill in Your Personal Info

  • Your name, address, Social Security number
  • Spouse info (if married)
  • Dependents (kids or others you care for)
💬 “Check every letter and number—tiny mistakes can slow things down.”
“Be careful when entering your info, especially your Social Security number. It’s like your tax fingerprint!”

Line 1a – Wages, salaries, tips

All money earned from jobs. Copy Box 1 from your W-2 forms.

💬 “Double-check all W-2s, add multiple jobs if needed, numbers must match.”
