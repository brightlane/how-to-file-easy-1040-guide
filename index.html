Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I file my taxes online?

Just follow the steps outlined in the guide! You can use the easy-to-follow instructions and click here to file your taxes online.

2. What documents do I need to file my taxes?

You will need your W-2 from your employer, any 1099 forms, and receipts for things you want to deduct (like donations or education expenses).

3. How do I get my refund?

If you’ve paid more taxes than you owe, the government will send you a refund. Follow the steps to ensure that you have all your info correct, and you will receive it soon!
