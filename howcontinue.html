Part 2 — Income

 
 
   
      Line 1a       Wages, Salaries, and Tips       💼          
   
     
       

Use Box 1 from your W‑2 forms and add all W‑2 wage amounts together.

     
   
 
 
   
      Line 8       Other Income, Including Freelance       🧾          
   
     
       

Use this catch‑all line for income that doesn’t fit elsewhere, including some freelance, gig, and crypto‑related items.

       
          Freelance or self‑employment income usually belongs on Schedule C first, then flows through Schedule 1 to Form 1040.        
     
   
 
 

Part 3 — Adjustments

 
 
   
      Line 12       Standard Deduction or Itemized Deductions       🧮          
   
     
       

For 2025, the standard deduction is $15,750 (single/MFS), $31,500 (MFJ/QSS), and $23,625 (HOH).

     
   
 
 
   
      Line 13       Qualified Business Income Deduction       🏪          
   
     
       

If you are self‑employed or run a pass‑through business, you may qualify for a QBI deduction.

     
   
 
 

Part 4 — Credits

 
 
   
      Credits       Child Tax Credit and Other Credits       👨‍👩‍👧‍👦          
   
     
       

Credits reduce your tax directly, so they can be more valuable than deductions.

     
   
 
 

Part 5 — Payments

 
 
   
      Line 25–33       Withholding, Estimated Tax, and Refundable Credits       💰          
   
     
       

Add up your federal withholding, estimated tax payments, and refundable credits to get your total payments.
